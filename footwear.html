How to Establish a Clothing Business

Steps to Establish a footwear Business

  1. Own a Shop: Choose a shop in the main footwear market.
  2. Take It on Rent: If buying is not feasible, rent a shop that fits your budget.
  3. Furnish the Shop: Design and furnish the shop according to your budget and theme.
  4. Purchase Goods: Buy clothing stock from the provided supplier addresses while staying within your budget.
  5. Marketing is Key: Advertise your shop as much as possible through online and offline methods.
